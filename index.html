The Trumbauersville Volunteer Fire Company was established in May 1906 and has provided excellent service and protection for over 114 years to our community 24 hours a day, 365 days a year. While our community has changed, the company's mission to serve and protect remains the same. Our service area includes
Trumbauersville Borough and portions of Milford, Richland and West Rockhill Townships. Our services include, but are not limited to fire, accident, rescue,
wildland protection, medical emergencies, disasters and other mishaps. In addition to emergency calls, we spend many hours training, holding fund raising
